Research focus

I became interested in how formal economic and international models often omitted behavioral drivers and institutional frictions. My research and practice focus on identifying those frictions and designing pragmatic interventions and evaluation strategies.

Selected topics I work on: evidence use, policy design under uncertainty, behavioral barriers to uptake, and institutional incentives.
