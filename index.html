About

I quickly became fascinated with how economic and international models were theorized without the behavioral factors that create them. Since then, my work has focused on the behavioral, economic, and structural barriers to improving policies and policymaking processes on micro to macro levels.

Below are just a few of the things (in no particular order) that I can’t help but think about when trying to tackle today and tomorrow's policy challenges:

Structures
Behaviors
