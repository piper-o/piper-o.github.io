Background

Grew up in Vermont, where I trained as a working student in competitive show jumping from age ten. I finished high school early to pursue it full time, kept at it through parts of college, and eventually chose to redirect that same level of commitment toward work with broader reach. Along the way: making sandwiches and homemade bread at the Red Onion Cafe, selling skis at swaps across the state, managing the finances of the only fully student-run coffee shop at Connecticut College.

These days I shoot film and digital photography, mostly portraits, street scenes, and urban decay. I restore found objects in wood, metal, and stone. I cook my way through whatever recipes friends and family are willing to share. Suggestions welcome.
